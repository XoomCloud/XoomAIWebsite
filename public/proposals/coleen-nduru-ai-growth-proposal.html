Call Summary

What we heard

The business model

You sell new home and land packages across multiple locations and builders, with a lead funnel driven mainly by Meta Ads, WhatsApp, web forms and your CRM, Kommo.

Many prospects need education, finance support and hand-holding before they are ready to move. The human sales conversation still matters.

The current bottleneck

Lead volume is building faster than the team can properly respond. Some leads are hot, some are early, and some are being mislabelled because the first interaction does not capture enough context.

The result: strong enquiries are getting buried, follow-up is inconsistent, and too much of your time goes into triage instead of sales.

Recommendation

Start with lead control, not more noise

Phase 1

Executive VA

The Executive VA sits above the lead flow and manages the administrative layer: inbox, CRM, WhatsApp-triggered workflows, follow-up, lead scoring and reminders.

  • Monitor new leads from Kommo.
  • Classify hot, warm and cold leads using your rules.
  • Trigger email, SMS and follow-up tasks.
  • Flag high-intent leads to Coleen immediately.
Phase 1

Call Agent

The call agent handles voice. It can answer inbound calls and, where appropriate, call warm opted-in leads who have already engaged with your ads or forms.

  • Qualify enquiries with a guided conversation.
  • Book appointments for suitable prospects.
  • Confirm meetings and reduce no-shows.
  • Hand over complex sales conversations to you.
Phase 2

Marketing Agent

Once lead handling is controlled, the marketing agent can improve Meta Ads performance and creative rotation without creating more manual pressure.

  • Review campaign and creative performance.
  • Refresh ads every 4-6 weeks.
  • Focus on higher-quality form completions.
  • Use data from Kommo to sharpen targeting.

Workflow

How it works day to day

Lead enters Kommo

A prospect clicks a Meta Ad, WhatsApp link or form. Their details land in Kommo as they do now.

XoomAgent™ reads the context

The Executive VA checks the source, available notes, form answers, keywords like pre-approval, location interest, builder/product fit and past conversation history.

The right action is triggered

Hot leads are escalated to Coleen. Warm leads receive immediate follow-up. Suitable opted-in leads can be sent to the call agent for qualification and appointment-setting.

Sales stays human

Once the lead needs finance advice, product guidance, confidence-building or negotiation, the AI gets out of the way and hands the conversation to you.

Kommo

Your CRM looks usable for automation

Based on the call, there is no immediate reason to throw Kommo out. The important part is not how pretty the browser interface is. It is whether the back end gives XoomAgent™ enough access to leads, pipelines, webhooks, call records and deal stages.

Kommo appears strong enough for the first implementation. The bigger issue is process design: making sure calls, notes, lead source, finance readiness, suburb preference and builder/product fit are captured consistently.

Setup focus

  • Define lead statuses and qualification rules.
  • Map source channels: Meta, WhatsApp, website and manual entries.
  • Connect call recordings and notes into the lead record where possible.
  • Create escalation rules for pre-approved or high-intent buyers.
