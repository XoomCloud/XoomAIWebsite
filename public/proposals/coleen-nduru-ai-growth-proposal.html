The outcome

One operating layer for research, leads and marketing

Remove the two biggest manual loads

The follow-up meeting identified two heavy workloads: searching builder portals and offers for a suitable package, and moving new enquiries into qualified conversations.

XoomAgent™ will organise both into repeatable workflows so current information and the next best action are available without starting from scratch each time.

Keep judgement with Coleen

The system will collect, compare, prepare and recommend. Coleen remains responsible for the client relationship, product suitability, advice and any final recommendation or commitment.

This preserves the trust that drives conversions while reducing research, triage, campaign administration and repetitive follow-up.

Proposed solution

Three connected workflows for the first month

Workflow 1

Builder portal intelligence

XoomAgent™ checks the approved builder and product portals on a scheduled basis, captures the latest offers and keeps a structured source library current.

  • Work across the agreed set of 20-30 portals where access allows.
  • Collect current brochures, PDFs, specials and package details.
  • Store source material in an approved Google Drive or Microsoft location.
  • Flag failed logins, changed pages and missing information for review.
Workflow 2

Property matching and shortlist

When Coleen provides a buyer brief, XoomAgent™ compares it with the current source library and prepares a shortlist for human review.

  • Read budget, location, finance, timing and package requirements.
  • Return the strongest three matches with reasons and source links.
  • Show gaps, assumptions and information requiring confirmation.
  • Leave final suitability and client advice with Coleen.
Workflow 3

Lead and marketing operations

XoomAgent™ connects new enquiries, Kommo follow-up and Meta performance into one managed lead workflow.

  • Triage enquiries and flag high-intent leads.
  • Qualify inbound and approved warm leads by phone, then book or hand over the conversation.
  • Prepare consent-based email, SMS and WhatsApp follow-up.
  • Review Meta campaigns and recommend creative or budget changes.
  • Re-engage approved Kommo segments through staged campaigns.

Guardrails

Automate preparation, not accountability

Human approval where it matters

XoomAgent™ can prepare a shortlist, message, campaign or call handoff. Coleen approves product advice, pricing, client commitments and any communication that carries material risk.

Consent-based outreach

The deployment will not cold-call scraped contacts. Outbound calling is limited to warm or opted-in leads. Email, SMS and WhatsApp activity will follow the approved audience, channel and consent rules.

The aim is a practical assistant Coleen can direct through Slack, with every workflow using approved systems, access controls and escalation rules.

Priority workflow

How property matching works day to day

Current source material is collected

On the agreed schedule, XoomAgent™ signs into approved portals, captures the latest available product documents and specials, and records the source date.

Coleen provides the buyer brief

Coleen supplies the buyer's budget, preferred location, finance position, timeframe, package requirements and any relevant constraints.

XoomAgent™ prepares the shortlist

The workflow compares the brief with current source material and returns the strongest three matches, the reasoning, source links and any information that still needs checking.

Coleen reviews and advises

Coleen validates the options, applies her commercial judgement and decides what is appropriate to present to the buyer.

Systems and access

Built around the tools already in use

The current split environment is workable: Microsoft 365 for email, Google Drive and Sheets for files, Kommo for lead and pipeline data, Meta for campaigns, and WhatsApp Business for customer conversations.

Connections depend on administrator access, available APIs and each vendor's security requirements. Where a direct connection is not available, XoomAI will confirm the safest supported alternative before that workflow is activated.

Initial access required

  • Dedicated XoomAI administrator accounts in Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace.
  • Approved Kommo access and pipeline definitions.
  • Meta API credentials generated through the documented process.
  • WhatsApp Business and RingCentral or equivalent SMS capability review.
  • Builder portal credentials and permission to use the source material.
