Proposed solution

A specialised 3-4 agent XoomAgent™ package configured for your gym, with phone, Meta, CRM workflow and personal VA capability.

Phone and receptionist agent

Inbound AI call handling through Aircall, with the ability to use a local number, forward your existing business mobile, or port a number into the system.

  • Inbound call answering and message capture
  • Lead pre-qualification and call routing
  • Call notes and details logged for follow-up
  • Escalation to you or the team when a human needs to step in

Meta ads agent

An agent for Facebook and Instagram campaign support, using the Meta API to help create, modify and analyse campaigns without living inside Ads Manager.

  • Create campaigns, ad sets and creatives from plain-English instructions
  • Adjust daily spend, audience, demographic and targeting settings
  • Analyse performance and shift attention toward stronger ads
  • Use brand rules and existing media assets for consistent creative

GymMaster workflow agent

GymMaster has a workable API, which gives us a strong base for automating lead, booking and member follow-up workflows.

  • Push lead form enquiries into GymMaster as prospects
  • Trigger booking reminders and trial-session instructions
  • Start follow-up loops for prospects who do not convert
  • Support reporting such as overdue payments or member status checks

Executive VA for Duncan

A personal virtual assistant for day-to-day admin, scheduling, task tracking and system queries across the connected business tools.

  • Ask operational questions in plain English
  • Request weekly or recurring reports
  • Draft messages, reminders and follow-ups
  • Use Discord or Slack on desktop and mobile as the command centre

Example workflow

1

Lead is generated

A person responds to a Meta lead ad, sends an enquiry, calls the gym, or comes through another agreed channel.

2

AI captures and qualifies

The relevant agent captures the details, responds quickly, books the next action where possible, and alerts you when a human sales call is required.

3

GymMaster is updated

The prospect is created or updated in GymMaster, with trial information, appointment details and follow-up status available to the team.

4

Follow-up keeps moving

If they attend but do not sign up, the follow-up loop starts automatically: check-in message, second-session prompt, and escalation if the conversation needs a person.

Important operating principle

Outbound AI sales is not the focus. Inbound handling, fast lead response, structured follow-up and admin automation are where the immediate return is strongest and safest.
