Outcome

One managed service across the business

The deployment combines the full eight-pillar XoomAgent™ workforce with two supermarket-specific roles for inventory/supplier operations and delivery/dispatch coordination.

Customers

Inbound and member communication

Handle common enquiries, triage calls, capture customer details, prepare responses and support consent-based outbound calls, SMS and email campaigns.

Growth

Marketing and customer database

Build repeatable weekly campaigns for new products, promotions and customer re-engagement using approved offers, lists and messaging.

Operations

Stock, suppliers and delivery

Monitor low-stock signals, prepare supplier comparisons and purchase recommendations, coordinate delivery jobs and flag delays or exceptions.

Priority workflows

Start where the operational load is highest

1

Reception and enquiry triage

Answer common questions, collect structured details and route matters to the right person. AI-assisted phone ordering is excluded from the launch scope because a supermarket catalogue creates too much product ambiguity for a good customer experience.

2

Member campaigns and promotions

Prepare and deliver approved SMS, email and call campaigns to customers with appropriate consent records. Cold outreach is not included without a confirmed legal basis and approved compliance controls.

3

Inventory and supplier support

Read approved stock data, identify reorder triggers, compare available supplier information and prepare purchase recommendations for human approval.

4

Delivery and exception coordination

Receive approved order data, prepare delivery tasks, monitor expected timing and notify staff or customers when a delay needs attention.

Implementation

Controlled onboarding before production

Onboarding includes

  • Business and workflow audit
  • Infrastructure and security setup
  • System access and integration configuration
  • Knowledge, approval rules and guardrails
  • Initial workflow builds and testing
  • Team training, launch and early tuning

Implementation sequence

  • 45-minute stakeholder scoping session after approval
  • Confirm systems, APIs, permissions and data owners
  • Select the first two to three production workflows
  • Build, test and validate with human approval points
  • Train users and move approved workflows into production
  • Monitor, tune and expand after launch
